This aircraft was built for the Flightgear flight simulator
using the program AC3D.
There are animations for primary flight controls
plus leading edge and trailing edge flaps,undercarriage ,
speed brakes and engine fan.


Ctrl+B activates and deactivates the speed brakes
G and Shift+G raises and lowers the landing gear.
] and [ lower and raise the leading and trailing edge flaps.

If you have any questions about this model please direct them
to the Flightgear mailing list.
